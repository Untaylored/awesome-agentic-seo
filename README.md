# Awesome Agentic SEO [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

> A curated list of tools where AI agents do the actual SEO and GEO work: research, write, optimize, publish, and track. Not just assist.

Most SEO software still waits for you to do the work. You pick the keywords, write the brief, approve the draft, and hit publish. The tools here don't wait. They run multi-step SEO and GEO work on their own, from market research to a live page, and several track whether AI engines cite you afterward.

This list only includes tools that execute. Assistants that sit idle until you paste a prompt do not qualify. See [Selection criteria](#selection-criteria) for the bar.

## Selection criteria

A tool earns a spot if it does at least one full job end to end with no human step in between.

Writing a draft after you paste a keyword is assistance. Researching a site, building a content plan, writing the article, and publishing it to a CMS on a schedule is an agent. GEO tools qualify when they monitor AI-search visibility and tell you what to change, not when they print a score once and stop.

Tools are listed alphabetically inside each section. Each description says what the tool does, in plain language.

## Autonomous content engines

Tools that take a site or a keyword set and publish finished SEO articles without a person writing each one.

- [Fonzy](https://fonzy.ai) - Researches your market, builds topic clusters, writes long-form articles, and publishes to WordPress, Shopify, or Webflow. Each piece is written to rank on Google and get cited by ChatGPT and Perplexity.
- [Koala](https://koala.sh) - Writes SEO articles using live SERP and real-time data, then publishes to WordPress in one click.
- [Outrank](https://outrank.so) - Plans, writes, and auto-publishes SEO articles to your CMS on a set schedule.
- [SEOBot](https://seobotai.com) - You give it a URL; it studies the site, builds a content plan, and ships articles weekly with internal links and images.

## GEO and AI search visibility

Tools that track how AI engines cite your brand and tell you how to show up more often.

- [AthenaHQ](https://athenahq.ai) - Tracks how often AI engines mention your brand and gives structured-data and content guidance to lift it.
- [Goodie AI](https://higoodie.com) - Monitors brand mentions and citations across AI search engines and flags where you are missing.
- [Otterly](https://otterly.ai) - Watches how your brand appears in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI answers, and tracks share of voice.
- [Peec AI](https://peec.ai) - Tracks visibility and competitor benchmarks across several large language models.
- [Profound](https://www.tryprofound.com) - Enterprise AI-search intelligence: large-scale prompt monitoring, citation analysis, and reporting on ChatGPT, Perplexity, and AI Overviews.
- [Scrunch AI](https://scrunch.com) - Makes site content easier for AI engines to read and cite, and tracks where you get pulled into answers.
- [Writesonic GEO](https://writesonic.com) - Adds AI-search visibility tracking on top of a content suite, so you can spot gaps and write against them in one place.

## Build your own

Platforms for assembling your own SEO agents instead of buying a fixed one.

- [AirOps](https://www.airops.com) - Build multi-step SEO content agents and workflows that run on your own data and brand rules.
